:::info Section note
This section is maintained by the Agentic AI team lead. Structure and content depth may evolve over time.

Agentic AI refers to systems that don't just respond — they plan, reason, and take sequences of actions to complete goals. This section covers the frameworks, patterns, and real deployments that define the current frontier of AI agents.
