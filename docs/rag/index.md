# Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

:::info Section note
This section is maintained by the RAG team lead. Structure and content depth may evolve over time.
:::

RAG grounds language models in your own data — preventing hallucinations and enabling accurate, source-backed responses. This section goes from foundational concepts to building and deploying production RAG pipelines.
